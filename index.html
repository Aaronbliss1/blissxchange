BlissXchange Terms of Service

1. Nature of Service

BlissXchange is a peer-to-peer (P2P) merchant platform operated by Bliss Intercambio Mercado Ltd, a company duly incorporated under the laws of the Federal Republic of Nigeria RC:7016422. We facilitate the exchange of digital assets for Nigerians.

2. Compliance Statement

While BlissXchange is a CAC-registered private limited company, we recognize the evolving regulatory landscape for digital assets. We strive to align our operations with the Nigeria Tax Administration Act 2026 and the guidelines set forth by the Central Bank of Nigeria (CBN) and the Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC) regarding Anti-Money Laundering (AML).

3. P2P Trading Protocol

  • First-Party Payments Only: You are strictly prohibited from paying from or receiving funds into a bank account that does not match your legal registered name.
  • Escrow System: BlissXchange acts as an intermediary. Once a trade is initiated, the digital assets is processed by BlissXchange and payment is made immediately.
  • Confirmation Timeframes: Sellers must confirm payments within 5 minutes from when transaction is completed.

4. Prohibited Activities

Users agree not to use BlissXchange for:

  • Triangular Arbitrage/Scams: Using BlissXchange to facilitate third-party fraudulent transfers.
  • Illicit Financing: Any activity related to money laundering or terrorism financing.
  • Platform Abuse: Attempting to scam BlissXchange customer supports.

5. Taxes and Reporting

Under the Nigeria Tax Administration Act 2026, users are responsible for their own personal income tax filings on profits made from crypto-to-fiat conversions. BlissXchange may be required by law to provide transaction summaries to the Federal Inland Revenue Service (FIRS) upon request.

6. Dispute Resolution

In the event of a disagreement, BlissXchange's dispute resolution team will act as the final arbiter. Users must provide valid proof (bank statements, screen recordings) to support their claims. By using this service, you agree to abide by the final decision of our moderators.

7. Limitation of Liability

BlissXchange is a facilitator and does not provide investment advice. We are not liable for:

  • Market volatility or price slippage.
  • Errors made by the user (e.g., sending funds to the wrong wallet address).
  • Temporary downtime of Nigerian banking networks or blockchain congestion.
